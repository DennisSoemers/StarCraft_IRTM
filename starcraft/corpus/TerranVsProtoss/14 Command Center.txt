<p><b>14 CC</b> is the most risky non-cheese opening a Terran player may choose to do. Although a rare opening, it is totally feasible depending on the circumstances and map. Generally this strategy is used on maps where Protoss players have a tendency to Fast Expand themselves. 
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Build_Order">Build Order</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=14_CC_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Build Order">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li> 9/10 - Supply Depot
</li>
<li> 14/18 - Command Center (at natural)
</li>
<li> Barracks
</li>
<li> Supply Depot
</li>
<li> Refinery
</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Build_Order_Clarification">Build Order Clarification</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=14_CC_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Build Order Clarification">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It is quite important to scout and see if the Protoss has also fast expanded. If the opponent:
</p>
<ul>
<li><i>Fast Expanded</i> - Then it is possible to skip Marine production and go straight to Factory units.
</li>
<li><i>2+ Gateway</i> - Then get a Bunker up, and a Tank out immediately. Make sure you repair the Bunker against Dragoon fire until the first Tank comes out.
</li>
<li><i>1 Gateway Tech</i> - This could suggest Reavers or Dark Templars, so get a fast Engineering Bay and play cautiously.
</li>
</ul>
