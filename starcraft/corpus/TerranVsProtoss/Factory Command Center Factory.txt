<p>Fact CC Fact, or 2 Factory timing after FE, is a timing attack generally performed by Terran in response to Protoss delaying their second gateway after expansion, whether it be for a Robotics Facility or an extremely greedy 3rd Nexus.
</p><p>This timing attack can either transition into a faster 3rd CC built directly on the 3rd expansion site or into building more factories to reinforce a stronger contain.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Build_Order">Build Order</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=Fact_CC_Fact&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Build Order">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Supply counts will vary a lot, therefore it is better to learn triggers rather than actual supply counts. There are also two variants, one that gets Siege Mode earlier on for an earlier contain, and one that researches Ion Thrusters earlier on and seeks to be more aggressive with vultures. Generally, both attacks seek to move out when the 2nd factory has completed with 6-8 marines, 3-4 tanks and vultures reinforcing.
</p><p>Against a Protoss that expands off 1-3 Dragoons, the Ebay can be delayed until after the 2nd Factory. From then on:
</p>
<ul>
<li>If you opened with a FD and did not lose too many marines, you may choose to not make more marines. Otherwise, you will want to train marines until you have a good enough amount to support your tanks, usually about 6-8.
</li>
<li>From the 1st Factory, continue to produce tanks.
</li>
<li>Start a 2nd Factory as soon as you have enough money.
</li>
<li>Start researching either Siege Mode or Ion Thrusters depending on the situation. If you opened Siege Expand, research Spider Mines.
</li>
<li>When you have 3-4 tanks, start to move out and reinforce with vultures. Bring 1 or 2 SCVs for repairs and to build Depots, Turrets, and Bunkers for a contain.
</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Micro">Micro</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=Fact_CC_Fact&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Micro">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The micro involved in pushing to the Protoss base is not much different from a 2 Factory All-in or a Strong FD. Marines are used for support and to take hits from Dragoons, and Vultures are used to lay mines aggressively in order to divert fire from the tanks.
</p><p>The more aggressive style involving Speed Vultures involves laying mines close to Dragoons in order to force the Dragoons to retreat or else risk losing all of them. If mines are laid close enough to the Dragoons and at the same time, Protoss cannot defuse the mines when they are planted. This allows the Terran to set up a positional advantage, as well as the Tanks to deal damage to the Dragoons. Usually Siege Mode is researched after Ion Thrusters in order to set up a more powerful contain along with bunkers and turrets.
</p><p>The second style where Siege Mode is researched first is usually used when Terran has set up a position in the beginning with mines covering potential flanks. When you have reached a close enough distance, siege the tanks and push slowly and contain with bunkers, turrets and depots. For a better example of this type of style, watch Flash vs BeSt on Fortress SE.
</p><p><br />
</p>
