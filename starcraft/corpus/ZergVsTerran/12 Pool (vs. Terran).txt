<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Overview">Overview</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=12_Pool_(vs._Terran)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Overview">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>12 Pool is a safer alternative to <a href="/starcraft/12_Hatch_(vs_Terran)" title="12 Hatch (vs Terran)" class="mw-redirect">12 Hatch</a> in ZvT, in particular it is highly effective in stopping <a href="/starcraft/8_Rax" title="8 Rax" class="mw-redirect">8 Rax</a> builds. However this safety comes at a price, as it lacks the vast larva benefits of a 12 Hatch.
</p><p>The 12 Pool is better at denying the opponents scouting <a href="/starcraft/SCV" title="SCV">SCV</a> than the 12 Hatch, but the later <a href="/starcraft/Zergling" title="Zergling">Zerglings</a> compared to <a href="/starcraft/9_Pool_(vs_Terran)" title="9 Pool (vs Terran)" class="mw-redirect">9 Pool</a> builds mean that they are unlikely to deal damage. Balancing <a href="/starcraft/Drone" title="Drone">Drones</a> and Zerglings may become difficult when playing versus rush builds.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Build_Order_Clarification">Build Order Clarification</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=12_Pool_(vs._Terran)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Build Order Clarification">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Mentality">Mentality</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=12_Pool_(vs._Terran)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Mentality">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>12 Pool is most known as a counter to a <a href="/starcraft/Bunker_Rush_(vs._Zerg)" title="Bunker Rush (vs. Zerg)">Bunker Rush</a>, and is not good against most of other strategies. If you opened with a 12 Hatchery build and scout a <a href="/starcraft/Barracks_Barracks_Supply_(vs._Zerg)" title="Barracks Barracks Supply (vs. Zerg)">proxy Barracks</a>, your Hatchery will likely be killed with Bunker Rush and it may be worth it to cancel your Hatchery and opt for a 12 Pool.
</p><p>Can be used for fast 2 Hatch Lair tech builds, which aim to attack the Terran early on with stronger units such as Mutalisks and Lurkers.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transition">Transition</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=12_Pool_(vs._Terran)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Transition">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Usually the fast 12 Pool 11 Gas build transitions into very fast 2 Hatch Mutalisks that can be used for very early game harass. If the timing is correct, the first mutalisks should pop out about the 5:30 minute mark and reach the Terran before he has Stim or Range upgraded. The primary goal of the harass is to kill as many SCVâ€™s as possible to economically catch up, because the build sets the Zerg player quite behind if the Terran did not opt for a Barracks first build and went for example one Barracks into Expansion. Another goal of the build can also be to commit fully to Mutalisks and try to kill the opponent with them alone. If Terran did 8 Barracks Zerg will likely come out on top anyway and will be able to make an easy transition to midgame (with for example Lurker/Ling).
</p>
