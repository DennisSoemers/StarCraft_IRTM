<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Overview">Overview</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=12_Hatch_(vs._Terran)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Overview">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>12 Hatch is used to gain a faster and stronger economy in the early game, allowing the player to pump Drones from two Hatcheries early on in order to transition into a better midgame. It is often considered the standard in ZvT, and its effectiveness has been proven over the years. From a 12 Hatch opening, a player can transition into a 3 Hatch build, followed by Mutalisk or Lurkers, or opt for a fast tech <a href="/starcraft/2_Hatch_Muta" title="2 Hatch Muta" class="mw-redirect">2 Hatch Muta</a> or Lurker build.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Build_Order">Build Order</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=12_Hatch_(vs._Terran)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Build Order">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>9 - Overlord
</li>
<li>12 - Hatchery (usually at the natural)
</li>
<li>11 - Spawning Pool
</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Build_Order_Clarification">Build Order Clarification</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=12_Hatch_(vs._Terran)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Build Order Clarification">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>After putting down the <a href="/starcraft/Spawning_Pool" title="Spawning Pool">Spawning Pool</a> it is time to decide whether to go with a 2 Hatchery or 3 Hatchery Lair build.
