<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Overview">Overview</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Overview">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In contrast to the popular <a href="/starcraft/3_Base_Spire_into_5_Hatch_Hydra_(vs._Protoss)" title="3 Base Spire into 5 Hatch Hydra (vs. Protoss)">3 Hatch Spire into 5 Hatch Hydra</a>, this build completely ignores early teching in favor for earlier hatches and drones. As a result, you will deal with most protoss openings through hydralisks instead of spire tech. Your midgame economy will be much stronger than usual, which will allow you to add on additional hatches rapidly. 
This build is very strong against the popular "neo bisu build", involving 2 gateways worth of zealots and 1 stargate worth of corsairs. Without early templar tech, the protoss is incapable of defending against your slightly earlier hydralisk production.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Basic_Build_Order">Basic Build Order</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Basic Build Order">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>9 Overlord
</li>
<li>9 Spawning Pool
</li>
<li>11 Hatchery, scout
</li>
<li>10 Six Zerglings
</li>
<li>13 Hatchery at your second natural*
</li>
<li>18 Overlord
</li>
<li>18 Hatchery in your natural for sim city
</li>
<li>27 Hatchery in your second natural for sim city
</li>
<li>26 Extractor
</li>
<li>25 Extractor
</li>
<li>24 Two Overlords
</li>
<li>@50 Gas - Hydralisk Den
</li>
<li>@100 Gas - Lair
</li>
</ul>
<p>(This is a variant off the original build order from LRM)Game. The original build order consisted of no drone scout and 8 lings instead of 6. It also consisted of not delaying the third base and cancelling it if protoss does 2gate. This build cuts drone production for a brief period to avoid needing to cancel the third hatchery vs a 2gate.)
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes">Notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Notes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>You want to delay putting down your third hatchery until you scout the protoss. If you see he is 1basing, place this hatchery at your natural and do not use this build. If it is a 2gate, spend your saved up larva on zerglings. If it is a 1gate tech, spend your saved up larva on drones.
</li>
</ul>
<p>If the protoss decides to pressure with 3 early Zealots or 2 Zealots and a Dragoon, you will be forced to make four more Zerglings before you place your fifth hatchery. If he does not pressure, avoid making any additional Zerglings.
</p><p>After you place your fifth hatchery, you must immediately make the two Extractors. Do not delay this at all. Furthermore, you must make sure your Hydralisk upgrades are constant.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Execution">Execution</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Execution">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Because you do not have Mutalisks to deal with early zealots, you must rely on sim city and sometimes Sunken Colonies to defend. There is a small window before all five hatcheries begin producing constant Hydralisks where you are vulnerable.
</p><p>You do not have Mutalisks to snipe templar, so you must rely on tactics with your Hydralisks. Position ~8 Hydralisks behind a moving protoss army and snipe templar. Do not get addicted to sniping templar or you will whittle down your own army. Do not engage a protoss army head on unless you have sniped a sufficient number of templar. Make sure your lurkers are coming out in good time or you can easily get run over.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Adaptation">Adaptation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Adaptation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="1_Base_Protoss">1 Base Protoss</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: 1 Base Protoss">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Do not use this build.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_Stargate_into_Templar_Tech">Early Stargate into Templar Tech</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Early Stargate into Templar Tech">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Make only a few hydralisks to hold off the corsairs. Making more does not serve any purpose, as early attacks will be shut down by storm. Make a few more rounds of drones and then start hydralisk production.
</p>
<h5><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_Stargate_into_Templar_Tech_with_a_fast_third">Early Stargate into Templar Tech with a fast third</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Early Stargate into Templar Tech with a fast third">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h5>
<p>You have two options here. You can flood with hydralisks to try to prevent the third, or you can ignore it and quickly take a fourth. Only try to bust it if his zealot count is low and you are confident in your storm dodging.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_Stargate_with_mass_Corsair_into_Templar_Tech">Early Stargate with mass Corsair into Templar Tech</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Early Stargate with mass Corsair into Templar Tech">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Make ~18 Hydralisks to hold off the Corsairs. Send Zerglings to all the possible places for him to expand. If you see he isn't expanding, expect a mass zealot followup. If he does expand, add on a few more hydralisks and take a fourth base. Alternatively, you can try to mass Hydralisks and shut down the third. However, if you lose your Hydralisks to storm, your Overlords will be exposed.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_Stargate_into_2-4_Gate_Zealot">Early Stargate into 2-4 Gate Zealot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Early Stargate into 2-4 Gate Zealot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>You will most likely need to build 1-2 sunks at your natural and third versus this build. Flood hydralisks as soon as your hydralisk den finishes. Once you have held off his attack and have a comfortable number of hydralisks, you can push out. If he didn't switch to templar tech fast enough or built a good number of cannons, you can end the game here. If he took a third and has no cannons or templars there, kill it. If he took a third and has cannons or templar there, do not try to break it as you will only waste. Instead, switch back to drone production and take a fourth while containing him. If he didn't try to take his third but has cannons or templar in his natural, switch back to drone production and take a fourth while containing him.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_Stargate_with_mass_Corsair_and_2gate_mass_Zealot_.28Neo_Bisu_Build.29">Early Stargate with mass Corsair and 2gate mass Zealot (Neo Bisu Build)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Early Stargate with mass Corsair and 2gate mass Zealot (Neo Bisu Build)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Make two sunks at your natural and third and flood hydralisks. Because his templar tech is very delayed, you can win in this situation simply by attacking once you have a large enough hydralisk force. Make sure you bring your overlords in the attack so spawning dark templar do not force you back.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_Stargate_with_mass_Corsair_and_Reaver">Early Stargate with mass Corsair and Reaver</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Early Stargate with mass Corsair and Reaver">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Keep at least six Hydralisks at each base to defend, do not move these unless the shuttle has died.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Double_Early_Stargate">Double Early Stargate</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Double Early Stargate">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Go kill him.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="No_Stargate_4gate_Speedzeal">No Stargate 4gate Speedzeal</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: No Stargate 4gate Speedzeal">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Make 2-3 sunkens at your natural and third and flood Hydralisks. Make sure you have overlords everywhere versus this build. You need to check if he is taking a third or adding on more gateways. If he continues adding on gateways, do not drone up and do not take a fourth. Focus on units and getting lurkers out as soon as possible. If he takes a fast third, you can either mass Hydralisks and try to bust it, or drone up and wait for lurkers to take a fourth.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Late_Game">Late Game</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Late Game">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Because of your excellent midgame economy, you can afford a large number of hatches. On four well-saturated bases, you can afford 10 hatches of Hydralisks, Zerglings, and Lurkers with room to continue expanding and teching. Because of your high hatchery count, it is a bad idea to quickly switch to Ultralisks, as many of the hatcheries will have idle larva.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Maps">Maps</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Maps">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Pro_features">Pro features</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Pro features">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>A close third. Versus early zealots, you will need to be able to quickly reinforce from one base to another.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Con_features">Con features</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=5_Hatch_before_Gas_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Con features">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>A far third.
</p>
