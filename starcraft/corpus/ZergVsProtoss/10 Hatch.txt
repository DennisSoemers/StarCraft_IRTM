<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Purpose">Purpose</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=10_Hatch&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Purpose">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This is one of the old school build openings against Protoss on 2 player maps where <a href="/starcraft/2_Gateway_(vs._Zerg)" title="2 Gateway (vs. Zerg)">2 Gateway (vs. Zerg)</a> is highly possible. However, due to increased skill of micro and faded out popularity of 2 gate this build is not common these days. This build gives you, compared to <a href="/starcraft/12_Hatch_(vs._Zerg)" title="12 Hatch (vs. Zerg)">12 Hatch</a>, earlier <a href="/starcraft/Natural_Expansion" title="Natural Expansion" class="mw-redirect">Natural Expansion</a> <a href="/starcraft/Hatchery" title="Hatchery">Hatchery</a> (by approximately 20 seconds) and faster <a href="/starcraft/Spawning_Pool" title="Spawning Pool">Spawning Pool</a> (by approximately 20 seconds), but it also gives you 3â€“5 seconds economy delay.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Build_Order_Clarification">Build Order Clarification</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=10_Hatch&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Build Order Clarification">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Scouting">Scouting</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=10_Hatch&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Scouting">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Scouting is optional, depending on walking distances and max player on that map count. The most standard is with the 10th drone you made before hatchery (4 player maps) or with the drone you made after pool (2 player maps).
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2nd_Extractor_trick">2nd Extractor trick</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=10_Hatch&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 2nd Extractor trick">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>You can chose to either do or not to do Extractor trick 2nd time. If you do, you will still have 3 Larvaes and an Overlord by the time Spawning Pool finishes. However, players don't agree if it's cost effective or not.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Gas_variation">Gas variation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=10_Hatch&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Gas variation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>If you are going for 2 Hatchery build you should make Extractor after you make the Overlord.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="3rd_Hatchery">3rd Hatchery</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=10_Hatch&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: 3rd Hatchery">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>If you are going for 3 Hatchery build and If you feel confident that your opponent isn't going <a href="/starcraft/2_Gateway_(vs._Zerg)" title="2 Gateway (vs. Zerg)">2 Gateway (vs. Zerg)</a>, you can put your 3rd hatchery as soon as you collect 300 minerals, this will happen when your <a href="/starcraft/Spawning_Pool" title="Spawning Pool">Spawning Pool</a> will be just about do be done.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="After_Spawning_Pool_is_done">After Spawning Pool is done</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=10_Hatch&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: After Spawning Pool is done">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After your Spawning Pool is done, you should already know your opponents build and should spawn Zerglings only if necessary. 
</p><p><br />
</p><p><br />
</p>
