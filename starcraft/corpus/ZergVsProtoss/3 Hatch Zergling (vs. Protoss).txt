<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Overview">Overview</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=3_Hatch_Zergling_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Overview">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>3 Hatch Zergling strategies are designed to throw the Protoss off balance by controlling the early game with larger than usual amounts of Zerglings.
</p><p>There are a couple of variations to this build, with 1 hatchery and 2 hatcheries.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Build_Order">Build Order</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=3_Hatch_Zergling_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Build Order">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="9_Pool_Speed">9 Pool Speed</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=3_Hatch_Zergling_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 9 Pool Speed">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<pre>   * 9/9 - Spawning Pool
   * 8/9 - Drone
   * 9/9 - Extractor
   * 8/9 - Overlord
   * 8/17 - Drone
   * @100% Extractor - send 3 Drones to mine gas
   * @100% Spawning Pool - 6 Zerglings
   * @88 Gas - take Drones off of gas one by one, giving you 104 gas. Research Speed.
   * Pump lings, after first overlord get more lings.
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Overpool">Overpool</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=3_Hatch_Zergling_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Overpool">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<pre>   * 9/9 - Overlord
   * 9/9 - Spawning Pool
   * 10/17 - Drone
   * 10/17 - Extractor
   * 10/17 - Drone, then stop Drone production and save up Larvae.
   * @100 Gas - take Drones off gas
   * @100% Spawning Pool - Research Zergling Speed
   * 10/17 - Six Zerglings
   * 13/17 - Six Zerglings 
</pre>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="10_Hatch">10 Hatch</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=3_Hatch_Zergling_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 10 Hatch">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>this is the slowest but the most effective
</p>
<pre>   * 9 - Gas Trick
   * 10 - Hatchery
   * 9 - Pool
   * 8 - Drone
   * 9 - Overlord
   * mass lings
   * 18 - Overlord 
   * mass lings
</pre>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Countered_By">Countered By</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=3_Hatch_Zergling_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Countered By">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

</li>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Counter_To">Counter To</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=3_Hatch_Zergling_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Counter To">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/starcraft/1_Gate_Tech" title="1 Gate Tech">1 Gate Tech</a> build orders that skimp on Zealots for faster tech.
</li>
<li><a href="/starcraft/Protoss_FE_(vs._Zerg)" title="Protoss FE (vs. Zerg)">Protoss FE</a> build orders that skimp on cannons.
</li>
<li>Poorly placed <a href="/starcraft/Sim-City" title="Sim-City">Sim-City</a>.
</li>
<li>Lazy or ineffective scouting.
</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notable_Maps">Notable Maps</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=3_Hatch_Zergling_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Notable Maps">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Strong">Strong</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=3_Hatch_Zergling_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Strong">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>3 Hatch Zergling strategies are usually best on flat maps, or maps that have wide open naturals that the Protoss Sim-City will have difficulty blocking. Examples:
</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/starcraft/Carthage" title="Carthage">Carthage</a>
</li>
<li><a href="/starcraft/Byzantium" title="Byzantium">Byzantium</a>
</li>
<li><a href="/starcraft/Destination" title="Destination">Destination</a>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Weak">Weak</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=3_Hatch_Zergling_(vs._Protoss)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Weak">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>3 Hatch Zergling strategies are generally weak on maps that have easy to block naturals. Examples:
</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/starcraft/Tau_Cross" title="Tau Cross">Tau Cross</a>
</li>
<li><a href="/starcraft/Blue_Storm" title="Blue Storm">Blue Storm</a>
</li>
<li><a href="/starcraft/Longinus" title="Longinus">Longinus</a>
</li>
</ul>
