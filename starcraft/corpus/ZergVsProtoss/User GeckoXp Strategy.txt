<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Categorize_Build_Orders">Categorize Build Orders</span></h3>
<p>We should try to categorize Build Orders. Our goal is to make a difference between macro oriented (standard) builds and exotic (cheese, all-in) build orders. We should try to also decide wether or not the strategies are still viable, or if they are outdated. If somebody has a suggestion how to realize this, I'll be happy to add a method here. 
</p><p>For now, simply post your findings in some way or form in the <a target="_blank" class="external text" href="http://www.teamliquid.net/forum/viewmessage.php?topic_id=436470#1">forum thread</a>. 
</p>
