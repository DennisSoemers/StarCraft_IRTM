<p>1 Rax gas is a popular opening for tech oriented builds. The build typically transitions into either a fast tank push, two port wraith, a fast drop build or more recently the <a href="/starcraft/Fantasy_Build" title="Fantasy Build">Fantasy Build</a>. 
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Mentality">Mentality</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/starcraft/index.php?title=1_Rax_Gas_(vs._Zerg)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Mentality">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The 1 Rax Gas build is extremely versatile and can transition into a myriad of different builds.  This usually means that the Terran opponent has the ability to keep the Zerg player guessing rather than the other way around.  This build is used to reach Factory level or higher tech quicker, which means that the Terran player is usually much more aggressive than the Zerg player until Lair tech.
</p>
